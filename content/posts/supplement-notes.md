# Methionine Metiyonin
Cheese  | 1.0g /100g.
Chicken | 0.8g /100g.

Supplementation from premade form does not matter much because supplementation
will not provide in terms of needed grams but miligrams.

Note that wikipedia reports 70kg person needs 1.33gr methionine.

# Carnitine
If taken >3g causes nausea, diarrhea, body fish smell, muscle weakness.

Daily supplement consumption of 0.5g-1g considered safe.

A human has ~20g carnitine in the body.
A human body synthesizes 11-34mg.
Meat consumer take around 150mg extra.
